# Product Overview

## What the product does

The product helps internal teams manage onboarding, documentation, and operational workflows in one place. It is intentionally centered on clear state, predictable handoffs, and easy-to-audit actions.

## Main areas

- Onboarding and training
- Documentation and knowledge search
- Operational workflows and approvals
- Support and escalation tracking

## New joiner mental model

- The UI is the entry point.
- Knowledge content lives in Markdown docs.
- Questions are routed before retrieval so the response format matches the task.
- Evidence is shown so answers can be reviewed quickly.

## Common terms

- `QA`: direct question answering over the indexed docs.
- `Summarize`: condense the most important points.
- `Compare`: contrast two or more documents or sections.
- `Extract`: pull out risks, action items, or next steps.
