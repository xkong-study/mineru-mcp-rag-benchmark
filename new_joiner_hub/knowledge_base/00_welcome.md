# Welcome

This hub is designed for the first week of a new joiner.

## Goals

- Help people understand how the company works.
- Give a single place to search onboarding, product, engineering, and support knowledge.
- Make it easy to move from "where is this documented?" to "what should I do next?"

## First week checklist

1. Read the product overview.
2. Review access and security basics.
3. Learn the engineering workflow and deployment process.
4. Find the support and escalation path.
5. Identify the primary people to contact for your function.

## Good questions to ask

- Where is the latest product overview?
- What do I need before I can access staging?
- How do I escalate a production issue?
- What is the code review and deployment flow?
