# Support and Escalation

## Support channels

- Use the team channel for normal questions.
- Use the incident channel for urgent production issues.
- Use the doc hub when you need a fast answer from internal documentation.

## Escalation matrix

1. Minor workflow question: ask the owner or team channel.
2. Blocked onboarding task: raise it with your manager or buddy.
3. Production impact: follow the incident process immediately.

## What to capture

- The issue summary.
- The environment affected.
- The exact time the issue started.
- Any screenshots or error messages.
